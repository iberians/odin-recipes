Pan de Bono (Colombian bread)

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of cassava flour (or cassava starch)
  2. 1 cup of grated fresh cheese (such as costeño cheese or any white cheese)
  3. 1/2 cup of milk
  4. 1 egg
  5. 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  6. 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  7. A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients:
    • In a large bowl, combine the cassava flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Add the wet ingredients:
    • Add the grated cheese, egg, milk, and melted butter to the flour mixture. Mix well until you have a smooth and homogeneous dough.
  4. Shape the balls:
    • With your hands, form small balls about the size of a golf ball and place them on a greased baking sheet or one lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake:
    • Bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until the breads are golden and cooked through.
  6. Serve:
    • Let them cool slightly and serve warm. They’re perfect with coffee or hot chocolate.
